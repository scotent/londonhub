THE HUB at Scotland House

Taking your business where it needs to be

ABOUT

Discover the Hub: a flexible home-from-home workspace opening in April 2017. With high quality facilities,a central location and competitive memberships, it takes your business where it needs to be while visiting the Capital. With more investors than any other city, it’s also the perfect platform to build your network and grow your reach.

FACILITIES & BENEFITS

Scotland House provides a base from which to network, make connections and grow your business. Offering state-of-the-art technology, Scotland House is as flexible as you need it to be, whether you want to host an event, use our meeting rooms or hot desks, while taking advantage of free Wi-Fi, tea and coffee. Our in-house team will be on hand to provide support, guidance and access to key partners, as well as offering an extensive programme of events.

LOCATION

Centrally located in the heart of the City and enjoying a prominent position on the banks of the River Thames. Excellent access to public transport with Blackfriars station only 300 metres away providing onward connections to London City in 32 minutes, Gatwick in 41 minutes and Euston in 16 minutes.
