  • ABOUT

    Scotland House is designed to create an innovative and collaborative environment in which Scottish companies can drive their growth, increase their footprint in London, and build their networks within the London business community through our programme of highly focused business events

  • FACILITIES

    With free WiFi, refreshments and access to onsite catering and business lounge facilities, members can meet, connect and do business with a dedicated front of house team to answer any and all queries. We also offer a range of hot desk and meeting room spaces for members to book, as well as a flexible event space with capacity for up to 80 people. Members can choose a membership package that is best suited to their individual needs.
